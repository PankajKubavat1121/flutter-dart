# Dart - Why Dart Interview Questions

## Q1. Why was Dart created?

**Answer:**

Google created Dart to build fast, modern, and cross-platform applications.

---

## Q2. Why does Flutter use Dart?

**Answer:**

Flutter uses Dart because it provides Hot Reload, fast compilation, excellent performance, and native compilation.

---

## Q3. Is Dart only used for Flutter?

**Answer:**

No. Dart can also be used for web, desktop, server-side, and command-line applications.

---

## Q4. Is Dart open source?

**Answer:**

Yes. Dart is an open-source programming language developed by Google.

---

## Q5. What are the advantages of Dart?

**Answer:**

- Easy to learn
- Fast
- Object-Oriented
- Null Safety
- Cross-Platform
- Strong Typing
